Guangyuan Shengyuan International Trade Co., Ltd. announced today a strategic partnership with three top international logistics providers. This collaboration is expected to reduce our average cargo transit time by 20% and significantly lower logistics costs for our clients.

"Efficient logistics is the lifeblood of international trade," said Mr. Xie Jinfu. "This partnership marks a significant step forward in enhancing our customer experience."
