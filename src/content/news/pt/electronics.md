As part of our business diversification strategy, Guangyuan Shengyuan has officially launched a new electronics export division. This division will focus on exporting high-quality Chinese-made smart home devices to South American and Southeast Asian markets.

Our catalog now includes smart security systems, energy-efficient appliances, and the latest mobile accessories. All products have passed international quality certifications.
